Teachable Machine Project

A no-code approach to build ML models

What is Teachable Machine?

Teachable Machine is a web-based tool by Google that lets anyone train simple machine learning models without coding. It supports images, sounds, and poses to help you explore how artificial intelligence can recognize patterns.

How It Works

  • Collect data with your webcam or uploaded files
  • Train a simple neural network using transfer learning in your browser
  • Test it live with predictions and confidence levels
  • Export models to web, Android, or iOS apps

How to Build Your Own

  1. Go to Teachable Machine
  2. Choose between Image, Audio, or Pose models
  3. Collect your own data samples
  4. Train until results are satisfactory
  5. Test with live predictions
  6. Export and integrate it in your apps

Benefits & Best Practices

  • Privacy: data stays in your browser
  • Fast learning: instant feedback loop
